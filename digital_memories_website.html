Website to store my memories

Read time 5 minutes

My reasoning for creating this webite:

I hear you asking, "Why did you create this website, and why call it memories?" Well to that I say, memories are the foundations of a persons personality, we are an amalgomation of all the people and things that we have encountred throughout our lives. So, I wanted to create a website in which I can store all my treasured memories without it taking up too much space on my phone (because I have a small amount of storage on my phone, lol)

No man is an island, entire of itself; every man is a piece of the continent, a part of the main -John Donne, 1624

My vision for this Memblog is to have all my memories stored on here through picture form. This will mainly be of places I have visited that filled me with an immense amount of joy, and the goal is to have it all in chronological order of year
